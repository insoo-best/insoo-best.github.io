About

I am currently a Ph.D. Student of Algorithmic Intelligence Laboratory (ALIN-LAB) at KAIST AI, advised by Prof. Jinwoo Shin, where I focus on Motion Deblurring for real-world applications. In parallel, I work as a Principal Researcher at the Samsung Advanced Institute of Technology (SAIT), focusing on generative models for image generation and processing. Previously, I worked at Mando as a Senior Researcher, developing automotive radar signal processing algorithms for autonomous driving and ADAS systems. Before that, I received my M.S. degree in KAIST EE, advised by Prof. Hoirin Kim, where I conducted my research in Statistical Speech & Sound Computing Lab (SSSC LAB), focusing on speech-based emotion recognition.
